Volunteering

Volunteering for Meals on Wheels is a great way to make a huge impact in a short amount of time. By delivering meals you bring nutritious food and a friendly visit to eight to 10 seniors and people with disabilities in your community. Anyone can deliver meals, and it’s a great volunteer opportunity for families and teams of employees, as well. Sign up today and find out how easy and rewarding it is to bring nutrition and independence to your neighbors!

Last year our volunteers made an amazing impact. What will you do this year?

Short Term Delivieries

The COVID-19 outbreak has created an immediate, short-term need to get more meals out to more seniors in your area. Delivery generally takes about 90 minutes to 2 hours, including pick-up and drop off of food bags at a central location. We could really use your help.

Call-In Program To Our Seniors

Our deepest thanks to everyone who has volunteered to place check-In calls to the seniors we serve. Your generosity is a ray of sunshine in these uncertain days. Due to the tremendous response, we are currently not accepting additional volunteers to place calls to seniors. However, we are still in great need of volunteer drivers to deliver meals to seniors in your community.
